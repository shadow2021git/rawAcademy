Driving Training

Driving Training Courses

Raw Academy has 3 year driving course that contains lot of vehicles such as Motorbikes, Motorbotes, Helicopters, Heavy Vehicles, Military Tanks. Every Raw soldier who is following this driving training course should train all of these vehicles. Let's see what are the contents of this training.

Call To Raw Driving Squad

Course Content

Every raw cadet should follow the basic weapon course. After the weapon training we choose 25 students to this driving course in each year. The appliers should be higher than 18 years old.

Motorbike Training

6 months
18 hours per day

  • Training is instructed by Major Mahish Bandara Denipitiya.
  • 8 trainers who was in the war train raw cadets with a hard training.
  • 2 phases called Forest Training, Night Training.
  • After the training cadet corps have to follow motorbote training course.

Motorbote Training

6 months

Heavy Vehicle Training

6 months
